# betafree-competitive-intelligence-analysis
Healthcare Market Intelligence project analyzing the Indian anti-glaucoma market through competitor analysis, market landscape, SWOT analysis, and strategic recommendations.
## Overview

This project presents a competitive intelligence analysis of **Betafree (Brinzolamide 1% + Brimonidine Tartrate 0.2%)** within the Indian anti-glaucoma market. The report evaluates the disease landscape, competitor positioning, market trends, pricing, and strategic opportunities using publicly available scientific literature, regulatory information, and healthcare market data.

The project demonstrates practical applications of healthcare market research, pharmaceutical competitor analysis, scientific literature review, and strategic business analysis.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze the global and Indian glaucoma treatment market.
- Evaluate Betafree's product profile and market positioning.
- Compare leading competitor brands based on composition, pricing, positioning, and company profile.
- Assess clinical evidence supporting Brinzolamide–Brimonidine combination therapy.
- Perform a SWOT analysis to identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.
- Generate strategic business recommendations based on market intelligence findings.
